Think Expedite

Expedite are a software development company based near Bristol, UK offering full-stack development services. The company has been working with a a multitide of clients from all business sectors including global brands like Dyson and JustEat. In the last 3 years the company has particularly focused on helping clients build microservice and serverless cloud architectures to manage large scale web traffic and IoT connectivity to connected consumer devices. The company was founded by Jon Reynolds in 2007. After reading a PhD in Biochemistry at the University of Bristol in 2000, Jon re-trained in web development from the ground up and now proactively enjoys working with clients to delivering solutions that meet their financial needs and expectations.
